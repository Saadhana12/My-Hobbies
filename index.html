My Hobbies

My favorite hobby is art. Art is really fun to do for me I like to paint, I like to draw, and I also like to color. I like all types of art!

Art has the power to educate people about almost anything. It can create awareness and present information in a way that could be absorbed by many easily. In a world where there are those who don't even have access to good education; art makes education an even greater equalizer of society.
